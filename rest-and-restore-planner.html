    Running On Empty Right Now?

    For the crisis-tired moments — when you can't do a whole plan, just do one of these.

    • ●Put your phone in another room and lie flat for 10 minutes. That's it.
    • ●Drink a full glass of water slowly and take 5 deep breaths.
    • ●Say no to the next ask. "I can't take that on right now" is a full sentence.
    • ●Step outside. Feel the air, the sun, the ground for two minutes.
    • ●Text one safe person: "I'm depleted. Can you just sit with me?"
    • ●Close your eyes and whisper, "God, I'm tired. Carry this for me."
    • ●Cancel one thing. The world keeps spinning. You're allowed.
