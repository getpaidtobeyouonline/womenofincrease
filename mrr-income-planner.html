What is Master Resell Rights?

When you buy a product with Master Resell Rights (MRR), you pay once for the rights — then you're free to sell it as your own, again and again.

Every sale you make is your sale. You keep 100% of what comes in. No splitting, no asking permission. One purchase, then your own income stream.
