    Small Ways to Give

    Generosity doesn't always cost money. Browse for a nudge.

    • ✦ Send an encouraging text to someone who crossed your mind.
    • ✦ Leave a genuine, specific compliment for a stranger or coworker.
    • ✦ Bring a meal or coffee to someone who's stretched thin.
    • ✦ Offer your time — babysit, run an errand, lend an ear.
    • ✦ Pass on clothes, books, or items you no longer use.

    • ✦ Tip generously, and add a kind note.
